From Farm to Cup

Founded in 2010 by Kalungi Isaac, Pearl Beans Coffee began with a simple vision: to share Uganda's exceptional coffee with the world while ensuring farmers receive fair compensation for their hard work.

Uganda, known as the "Pearl of Africa," produces some of the world's most unique coffee beans. However, many farmers lacked access to international markets and received only a fraction of their coffee's true value.

Today, we work with over 250 farming families across Uganda's prime coffee-growing regions, providing training in sustainable farming, fair trade certification, and direct access to global specialty coffee markets.

Our name combines "Pearl of Africa" with "Beans" to represent our commitment to showcasing Uganda's treasure through its most valuable agricultural product.
