Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place a wholesale order?

For wholesale orders, please create an account on our website and apply for wholesale pricing. Once approved, you'll be able to place orders directly through your account dashboard. Minimum order quantity for wholesale is 10kg.

What are your shipping times and costs?

We offer free shipping on orders over $100. Standard shipping takes 7-14 business days internationally. Express shipping (3-7 days) is available at an additional cost. All shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your location and order size.

How can I become a Pearl Beans farmer?

We're always looking to partner with quality-focused farmers. Please email farmers@pearlbeanscoffee.com with details about your farm location, size, current practices, and coffee varieties grown. Our team will schedule a farm visit and assessment.

Do you offer samples for roasters?

Yes, we offer sample packs for professional roasters. Please contact us at wholesale@pearlbeanscoffee.com with your business details and roasting volume. We'll arrange a sample pack with our current harvests.
