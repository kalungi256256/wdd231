NP Membership Benefits

Our NP Membership is designed specifically for non-profit organizations with no membership fee.

  • Listing in our online directory
  • Access to member-only resources
  • Monthly newsletter subscription
  • Participation in community events
  • Networking opportunities
  • Volunteer coordination support

Cost: Free for qualifying non-profit organizations

×

Bronze Membership Benefits

Our Bronze Membership provides essential benefits for small businesses and startups.

  • All NP Membership benefits
  • Business listing with enhanced visibility
  • Access to business development workshops
  • 10% discount on chamber events
  • Monthly business spotlight opportunity
  • Access to member referral program
  • Basic advertising in chamber publications

Cost: UGX 200,000 per year

×

Silver Membership Benefits

Our Silver Membership offers enhanced benefits for growing businesses.

  • All Bronze Membership benefits
  • Priority listing in our directory
  • 20% discount on chamber events
  • Featured business spotlight quarterly
  • Access to exclusive networking events
  • Business training and development sessions
  • Enhanced advertising in chamber publications
  • Access to market research data

Cost: UGX 400,000 per year

×

Gold Membership Benefits

Our Gold Membership provides premium benefits for established businesses.

  • All Silver Membership benefits
  • Premium placement in our directory
  • 30% discount on chamber events
  • Monthly featured business spotlight
  • VIP access to all chamber events
  • Priority sponsorship opportunities
  • Full-page advertising in chamber publications
  • Access to exclusive business leads
  • Complimentary booth at annual business expo
  • Leadership opportunities within the chamber

Cost: UGX 750,000 per year
