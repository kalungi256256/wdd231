About Mukono

Mukono is a vibrant town in the Central Region of Uganda, located approximately 21 kilometers east of the capital city, Kampala. Known for its strategic location along the Kampala-Jinja highway, Mukono serves as a crucial commercial and educational hub in Uganda.

The town is home to Uganda Christian University, one of the country's premier institutions of higher learning, attracting students from across East Africa. This academic presence contributes to Mukono's dynamic and youthful atmosphere.
