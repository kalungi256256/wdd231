Site Name

Pearl Beans Coffee

This name combines "Pearl of Africa" (Uganda's famous nickname) with "Beans" to represent premium Ugandan coffee. The name was selected to create a memorable brand that honors Uganda's identity while being internationally recognizable. "Pearl" evokes quality, rarity, and value, while "Beans" clearly communicates the coffee focus. This creates an elegant yet approachable name that appeals to both specialty coffee markets and casual consumers seeking authentic African coffee.

Site Purpose

Uganda Coffee Connect serves as a comprehensive digital marketplace and information hub that bridges the gap between Ugandan coffee farmers and the global coffee market. The site provides detailed information about Uganda's coffee-growing regions, different coffee varieties (Robusta and Arabica), farming practices, and sustainability initiatives. Visitors can browse and purchase premium Ugandan coffee beans directly from cooperatives and individual farmers, access educational content about coffee cultivation and processing, view farmer profiles and their stories, and learn about fair trade practices. The site aims to empower local farmers with better market access while educating international buyers about the quality and origin of Ugandan coffee.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: What types of Ugandan coffee are available, and what are the flavor profiles of each variety?

A specialty coffee roaster from Europe wants to source high-quality Ugandan coffee for their shop. They need detailed information about the different coffee varieties grown in Uganda (Robusta vs. Arabica), regional flavor characteristics, processing methods (washed, natural, honey), and how these factors affect the final cup profile.

Scenario 2: How can I purchase coffee directly from farmers, and what are the pricing and shipping options?

A coffee enthusiast interested in ethical sourcing wants to buy directly from Ugandan farmers. They need clear information about available products, pricing per kilogram, minimum order quantities, payment methods, international shipping options and costs, and estimated delivery times to their location.

Scenario 3: What sustainability and fair trade practices do the coffee farmers follow?

A socially conscious buyer wants to ensure their purchase supports sustainable farming and fair compensation for farmers. They need information about organic certification, environmental practices, fair trade partnerships, farmer cooperative structures, and how much of the purchase price goes directly to the farmers.

Color Scheme

The color palette combines sophisticated dark blue tones with warm coffee accents for a premium, modern aesthetic:

Primary Color

#0A1628

Usage: Headers, navigation, footer backgrounds, primary buttons - deep midnight blue for sophistication

Secondary Color

#D4A574

Usage: Call-to-action buttons, accents, highlights, borders - warm golden coffee tone

Accent Color

#1E3A5F

Usage: Links, hover states, subheadings, icons - rich navy blue for depth

Background Color

#F5F5F5

Usage: Page backgrounds, card backgrounds, section dividers - clean light gray

Rationale: The dark blue-black primary color creates a premium, sophisticated atmosphere that elevates the coffee brand. The warm golden secondary color represents quality coffee and provides visual warmth against the cool dark blue. The navy accent adds depth while maintaining the professional tone, and the light neutral background ensures excellent readability and modern appeal.

Typography

Heading Font: Playfair Display

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog

Usage: All headings (h1, h2, h3), page titles, and major section headers

Weights: 600 (Semi-bold) and 700 (Bold)

Rationale: Playfair Display is a sophisticated serif font that conveys elegance and adventure. Its distinctive character adds personality while remaining highly readable for headings.

Body Font: Inter

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. This versatile sans-serif font ensures optimal readability across all devices and screen sizes, making it perfect for body text, navigation menus, and user interface elements.

Usage: All body text, paragraphs, navigation links, buttons, and form elements

Weights: 400 (Regular), 500 (Medium), 600 (Semi-bold)

Rationale: Inter is a highly legible sans-serif font designed specifically for computer screens. Its excellent readability at small sizes and clean, modern appearance make it ideal for all body content.

Layout Description:

  • Team/Products Section: Grid of images (mix of square and circular) with titles, descriptions, and "Read More" buttons
  • Services Section: Cards with icons, headlines, and descriptive text, plus a client section with testimonial placeholders
  • Footer: Quick links, contact information (address, email), and social media icons
