Our Processing Methods

Washed Process

Clean, bright flavors with consistent quality. Beans are fermented and washed to remove fruit.

Natural Process

Fruit-forward, complex flavors. Beans dry inside the coffee cherry for 3-4 weeks.

Honey Process

Sweet, balanced profile. Some fruit pulp remains during drying for enhanced sweetness.
